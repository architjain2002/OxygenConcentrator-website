About Our product

Our Product

In our world, amidst many health issues to survive, we need oxygen going from our lungs to the cells in our body. Sometimes the amount of oxygen in our blood can fall below normal levels. Asthma,Pneumonia,pulmonary hypertension,lung cancer, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), the flu and recently COVID-19 are some of the health issues that may cause oxygen levels to drop. When the levels are too low, we may need to take extra oxygen, known as oxygen therapy.They require machines which produces oxygen to recover people. Now a days, we could observe that there is a scarcity of oxygen tanks and concentrators and many were unable to buy them. So to make it affordable we are presenting our idea using polymer generated model.
